About Bossman

Bossman Experiences brings food, music and community together through high-energy pop-up events across Western Sydney.

We build events that feel easy to attend, fun to share and packed with flavour. Western Sydney Winterfest is designed for families, mates, food lovers and anyone keen for a night out without the stress.

What to expect

  • Curated street-food vendors with savoury, sweet and drink options.
  • A family-friendly setup with space to walk, eat and hang out.
  • Free entry and free parking with more than 500 spots available.
  • Giveaways, photo moments and a lively winter festival atmosphere.

Event FAQ

Quick answers before you pull up to Western Sydney Winterfest.

Is entry free?

Yes, entry to Western Sydney Winterfest is free.

Is parking available?

Yes, there is free parking with more than 500 spots available at the venue.

Where is the event?

Richmond Raceclub, 312 Londonderry Rd, Londonderry NSW 2753.

What are the opening times?

Friday 5pm to 10pm, Saturday 12pm to 10pm, and Sunday 12pm to 8pm.

Is it family-friendly?

Yes, the event is designed to be relaxed, welcoming and suitable for all ages.

Can I bring my dog?

Assistance animals are welcome. For pets, please check venue guidance before attending.
